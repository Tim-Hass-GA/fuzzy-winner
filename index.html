About the Game

Your dog Brian likes to have a clean area to run, play, and jump in. The objective of the game is to keep the area clean, so you and Brian can have a nice place to have fun

  • directions_run Use the keyboard to move the player around the board.
  • directions_run Collect as may objects as you can, once you've used all your poop bags you'll need to dispose of them.
  • shopping_basket Take your "collected" objects to the "proper disposal" area.
  • warning Avoid running into static objects.
  • mood Obtain 1000 points to win the game.
